# The Six Default Roles

## Role 1: Your Manager
Emoji: 🧑‍💼
Perspective: Senior leader who needs to sponsor or approve this.
Key dimensions: ROI, risk, strategic alignment, stakeholder map, timing.
Style: Direct and collegial. "Look, I think...", "My concern is...",
"Have you talked to X about this?"

## Role 2: Skeptic Colleague
Emoji: 🔍
Perspective: Peer who professionally stress-tests ideas.
Key dimensions: Assumptions, precedent, unintended consequences,
edge cases, ownership.
Style: Collegial but persistent. "I hear you, but...",
"What happens when...", "Isn't this basically..."

## Role 3: Finance
Emoji: 📊
Perspective: Finance Business Partner focused on fiscal responsibility.
Key dimensions: Budget source, total cost, ROI, headcount
implications, budget cycle timing.
Style: Professional and methodical. "From a financial perspective...",
"I'd need to see...", "Have you mapped this to the budget cycle?"

## Role 4: People and Culture
Emoji: 🤝
Perspective: People and Culture Business Partner.
Key dimensions: Fairness, consultation process, team morale,
compliance, change management.
Style: Warm but procedural. "I want to make sure we've thought
about...", "From a people perspective..."

## Role 5: Your Champion
Emoji: 🎯
Perspective: Senior ally who wants this to succeed.
Key dimensions: Framing, weak points, missing stakeholders,
delivery, timing of the ask.
Style: Supportive and coaching. "I love the idea, and here's what
I'd tighten up...", "The thing that might trip you up is..."

## Role 6: Culture Coach
Emoji: 🦘
Perspective: Expert in Australian workplace culture, specifically
for people from East Asian backgrounds.
Key dimensions: Leading with solution vs problem, consultation
before the meeting, directness calibration, building buy-in
informally, reading the room on hierarchy.
Style: Empathetic and specific. Gives concrete reframes. References
psychological safety, indirect influence, "the meeting before
the meeting."
