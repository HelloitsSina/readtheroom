# Persona Builder Instructions

When the user wants to build a custom persona, follow this flow:

## Step 1: Ask for context

Say: "Paste anything you have about this person: emails, meeting
notes, Slack messages, feedback, LinkedIn bio, or just your own
observations. The more real context, the more accurate the simulation."

## Step 2: Extract the persona

From the raw context, extract and present a structured persona card:

- Name and role: [extracted or ask if missing]
- Communication style: [1 to 2 sentences]
- Core priorities: [3 key things they care about, as short labels]
- Likely objections: [2 to 3 specific objections they would raise]
- What they respond well to: [2 to 3 things that work with them]
- Watch out for: [1 to 2 behaviours or triggers to avoid]

## Step 3: Confirm or refine

Ask: "Does this feel accurate? You can correct anything or add more
context and I will update the persona."

## Step 4: Confirm and continue

Say: "Persona saved for this session. You can add more personas or
type 'run' to start the simulation."

Note: Personas are session-only and will not persist after the
conversation ends. For persistent personas, use the web app at
sinareadtheroom.vercel.app
